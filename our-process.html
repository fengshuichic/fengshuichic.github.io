Our Process

1

Reflection

True happiness comes from accomplishing your life goals. Some people know their purpose naturally, while others search extensively. We begin with personal reflection on your core values — covering your personal aspirations, surrounding spaces, and relationships.

2

Engagement

Our practitioners listen carefully to your aspirations and study your physical spaces, floor plans, and furniture arrangements. We apply traditional Feng Shui principles customized to your individual situation, creating a personalized guide to maintaining positive Qi flow.

3

Growth

The Feng Shui Chic Design Brief™ provides clear implementation steps and guidelines. As you implement our recommendations, you'll gain confidence and empowerment — seeing tangible results in your daily life.

4

Thrive

We forge meaningful connections with our clients. The Design Brief becomes the keys to your prosperity and happiness, with ongoing support available as your life evolves and changes.
