What is Feng Shui?

Feng Shui is an ancient Chinese system that examines the design and layout of buildings and structures, the individuals who reside in them, the local physical environment, and how the flow of energy known as Qi (pronounced "chee") interacts within all of these elements.

The Meaning

The terminology derives from Chinese: "Feng" translates to "wind" while "Shui" means "water." Historical characters visually represented a bird in flight and flowing water, embodying the dynamic nature of energy movement.

The Practice

The practice of Feng Shui can serve to both analyze and remedy the physical environments we create for ourselves. How Qi energy flows through your home, office, or business can significantly affect your happiness and success — and it's something that you can impact.

For centuries, practitioners have applied Feng Shui principles to enhance various life domains including health, wealth, careers, relationships, and marriages.
