Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Feng Shui Chic. The company values visitor privacy and this policy summarizes our information collection practices. This privacy policy does not govern the collection and use of information by companies that Feng Shui Chic does not control.

2. Handling of Personally Identifiable Information

The company collects data including names, emails, phone numbers, addresses, user IDs, passwords, browser information, IP addresses, and session data. Information is protected through commercially reasonable security measures. We will not sell or rent it to a third party without your permission.

The company may use contact information for service updates unless users opt out. Data disclosure occurs only as needed to provide services, comply with law, protect rights and safety, enforce terms, or prevent fraud.

3. Other Information Collection

Non-identifying information like domain names and IP addresses may be collected for technical administration, research, customer service, and marketing purposes.

4. Cookies

Feng Shui Chic uses cookies to store personal data on your computer. Browser cookie restrictions may limit website functionality.

5. External Data Storage

We may store your data on servers provided by third party hosting vendors with whom we have contracted.

6. User Privacy Responsibilities

Users should protect passwords, log off after sessions, install antivirus software, and notify the company via the Contact page if accounts are compromised.

7. Non-U.S. Users

The information will be transferred out of your country to the United States. Users consent to this by using the service.

8. Children's Information

Feng Shui Chic does not knowingly collect information from children under 13, per COPPA requirements.

9. Policy Changes

The company reserves modification rights for legal or business reasons.

10. Contact Information

Questions should be directed to us via the Contact page.
